CASE NO. CIB-2026-0031
04.14.2026 Completed
Operation Codename
Operation Iron Curtain
Location East Los Santos
Commanding Officer Capt. Elias Makya
Duration 3 Hours 42 Min
Clearance Level Level 3
Divisions Involved
SWAT Criminal Investigation Division Interceptor Unit
Operation Overview

Following a three-week surveillance operation targeting a known narcotics distribution hub in the East Los Santos warehouse district, CIB Command authorized a joint-entry raid under Operation Iron Curtain. Intelligence gathered by the Criminal Investigation Division confirmed the presence of armed gang members and a significant quantity of contraband within the premises.

SWAT units breached the primary entrance at 02:14 hours while the Interceptor Unit maintained a perimeter along all exit corridors. CID investigators accompanied the tactical teams to secure evidence and manage detainee processing on-site.

Operation Outcome
  • 7 suspects apprehended, 2 outstanding warrants cleared
  • Approximately 14kg of narcotics seized and transferred to evidence lockup
  • 3 illegal firearms recovered, including one unregistered automatic rifle
  • No CIB personnel injuries sustained during the operation
  • Case forwarded to the District Attorney's office for prosecution

CASE NO. CIB-2026-0047
04.18.2026 — PRESENT Ongoing
Operation Codename
Operation Sandstorm
Location Blaine County / Route 68
Commanding Officer Capt. Elias Makya
Duration Active — Day 3
Clearance Level Level 4
Divisions Involved
Gang Recon Division Heat Unit Interceptor Unit Criminal Investigation Division
Operation Overview

Operation Sandstorm is an ongoing multi-agency interdiction effort targeting a narcotics pipeline confirmed to be running through the Route 68 corridor between Sandy Shores and the Los Santos metropolitan distribution network. Gang Recon Division intelligence identified the supply chain following a six-week undercover surveillance operation embedded within the rural distribution cells.

The Heat Unit is currently conducting aerial surveillance and vehicle interdiction along the primary corridor while the Interceptor Unit manages ground-level stop operations. CID investigators are building the evidentiary package required for federal prosecution of the organizational leadership.

Progress Report — As of 04.18.2026
  • 4 couriers intercepted, charges pending with the DA's office
  • Estimated 22kg of narcotics seized across three vehicle stops
  • Primary distribution cell identified — warrant application in progress
  • Operation is active — further updates to follow upon conclusion
