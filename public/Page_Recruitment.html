Eligibility Criteria

Application Requirements

Requirements vary depending on your home agency. Review the criteria for your department carefully before submitting your application.

Accepting Applications From
Los Santos County Sheriff
LSCS
Rank & Service Requirements
  • Must hold the rank of Deputy Sherriff Bonus 1 within the LSCS
  • Minimum of 30 days of active service within the department
  • Must have completed the LSCS Field Training Program
  • No active disciplinary actions or suspensions on record
Performance Standards
  • Demonstrated proficiency in report writing and incident documentation
  • At least 10 logged arrests or detainments in the past 30 days
  • No pattern of excessive force complaints on file
  • Supervisor endorsement letter required at time of application
Additional Criteria
  • Must be in good standing with the LSCS command structure
  • Ability to operate in plainclothes and undercover capacity
  • Willingness to collaborate with inter-agency personnel
  • Applicants on probationary status are not eligible

Accepting Applications From
Los Santos Police Dept.
LSPD
Rank & Service Requirements
  • Must hold the rank of Officer or above within the LSPD
  • Minimum of 30 days of active service within the department
  • Must have completed the LSPD Academy and Field Training
  • No active disciplinary actions or suspensions on record
Performance Standards
  • Demonstrated proficiency in investigative procedures and evidence handling
  • At least 10 logged arrests or detainments in the past 30 days
  • Clean use-of-force record with no open investigations
  • Supervisor endorsement letter required at time of application
Additional Criteria
  • Must be in good standing with the LSPD command structure
  • Prior detective or investigative unit experience is a strong advantage
  • Willingness to operate across jurisdictions as required by CIB
  • Applicants on probationary status are not eligible
