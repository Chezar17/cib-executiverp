Gang Recon Division
G.R.D
The Gang Recon Division is responsible for the surveillance, infiltration, and intelligence gathering of organized criminal gangs operating within San Andreas. GRD Detectives embed themselves within communities to monitor gang activity and disrupt criminal networks before they escalate.
  • Gang threat assessment & mapping
  • Undercover infiltration operations
  • Inter-gang conflict early warning
  • Surveillance on the street-level

Criminal Investigation Division
C.I.D
The Criminal Investigation Division handles complex criminal cases including organized crime, financial fraud, murder, and high-profile felonies across San Andreas. CID agents work directly on building prosecutable cases, managing informants, and coordinating arrests with field units.
  • Homicide & major crimes investigation
  • Financial crime & money laundering
  • Witness protection & informant management
  • Court liaison & evidence management
