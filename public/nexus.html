Classified Registry · Handler Access Only
CIB Informant Registry
Secure database of all active, inactive, and burned informants currently registered with the Central Investigation Bureau. Includes handler assignments, gang affiliations, active tasking, and handler notes. Identities are strictly classified. Access is restricted to the assigned handler and CIB Command only. Unauthorized disclosure of informant identities is a federal offense and may endanger lives.

Investigation Reports
Secure repository for all formal Investigation Reports filed by CIB detectives. Each report covers the complete documentation of a criminal case including victim and suspect details, witness affidavits, forensic evidence, autopsy results, and final case closure. Reports can be created, edited, and exported as official PDF documents matching the CID Form 0001 standard format.
